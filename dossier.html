Corz

Corz is a versatile druid who is equally at home in a healing or damage-dealing role. Despite being a fierce competitor in raids, Corz is known for his calm demeanor and positive attitude. In his spare time, he enjoys exploring Azeroth's many hidden nooks and crannies and is always on the lookout for rare herbs and other valuable resources.

Rekz

Rekz is the stalwart tank of the Meow Meow Kitties. His ability to soak up damage and hold the attention of multiple enemies at once has been instrumental in the guild's success. While some may view tanking as a thankless job, Rekz takes great pride in his ability to protect his friends and lead his guild to victory.

RB

RB is a skilled marksman who takes great pleasure in taking down difficult enemies from afar. His trusty pet, a fierce raptor named Razorclaw, is a constant companion on his many hunting expeditions. RB's love of the hunt is matched only by his fierce loyalty to his guildmates, whom he considers family.

Otter

Otter is a hunter who specializes in taming rare and exotic beasts. His collection of pets is the envy of many in the guild, and he takes great pride in showing them off. While he can be somewhat reserved at times, Otter is fiercely loyal to his guildmates and will go to great lengths to protect them.

Nat

Nat is a holy warrior who fights with both faith and ferocity. Despite being known for his tendency to "pad" his damage meters, Nat's dedication to his guildmates and his willingness to put himself in harm's way have earned him the respect of his peers. Outside of raiding, Nat is a devout follower of the Light and spends much of his time in prayer and meditation.

Turbo

Turbo is a master of the arcane who delights in unleashing devastating spells on his enemies. His quick wit and playful demeanor make him a popular member of the guild, and his love of pranks and mischief often keeps his guildmates on their toes. Despite his mischievous streak, Turbo is a dedicated member of the Meow Meow Kitties and is always willing to lend his considerable magical talents to the cause.
